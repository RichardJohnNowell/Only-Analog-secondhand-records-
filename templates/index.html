Introduction

  Only Analog (OA) is a secondhand records and cassettes stall in St. Nicholas Market, Bristol BS1. The title Only Analog comes from our policy of selling music that is an analog format rather than digital, such as cds. Our logo 'Smooth not brittle' reflects how the music sounds - analog is smooth while digital is brittle. The original idea for Only Analog came about when talking to djs and music lovers about how they preferred the smoothness, or continuity, of the sound. The website page 'Opinions' explores these ideas; the pros and cons of each format.

Record Selection


  As you can see from the pictures above, Only Analog have a records stock that covers a broad range of tastes. There are some types of music such as classical or easy listening which are however absent by choice. The condition of the records themselves is good-to-excellent and each one has been cleaned in a professional records cleaning device.


St. Nicholas Market, Bristol

 Only Analog's home is in St. Nicholas Market (St. Nick's), which has existed since 1743. It is located on Corn Street in the centre of Bristol and was named Britain's Best Large Indoor Market in 2016.


St. Nicks has Bristol's largest collection of independent retailers, with stalls as diverse as: books, ceramics, clothing, fabrics, jewellery, paraphenalia, sauces, singing bowls and so forth: the place to trade! There are also many stalls selling food with flavours from all around the world! Yum!
